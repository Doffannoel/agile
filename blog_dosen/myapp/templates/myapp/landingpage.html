About Me

I am Sesaria Kikitamara

Sesaria is a faculty member of Software Engineering Universitas Pasetiya Mulya. Her research interests are mainly focused on Blockchain, User Experience, and Software Project Management. During her time in university, she was active to serve as a teaching assistant.

This experience motivated her to be a lecturer. Furthermore, she also wants to be an inspiration to encourage more women in computer science by the fact that only a few women choose this field as their career. Despite teaching, learning, and research, she also loves to play piano and join volunteering activities.
